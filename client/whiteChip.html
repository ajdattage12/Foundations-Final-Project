White Chip Macadamia Nut

Ingredients

  • 2 sticks salted butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups cane sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla
  • 3 cups white flour
  • 1 cup white chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped macadamia nuts

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F
  2. Combine eggs, butter, cane sugar and vanilla into mixing bowl.
  3. Mix until creamed together and sugar is no longer granulated.
  4. In a seperate bowl, combine all dry ingredients and mix together.
  5. Slowly add dry ingredients to creamed wet ingredients.
  6. Mix well. Stir in the white chocolate chips and macadamia nuts.
  7. Use 1 inch cookie scoop and drop onto ungreased pans.
  8. Bake for about 10 minutes or until edges are golden brown.
  9. Remove from pan and allow to cool on wire baking rack.
