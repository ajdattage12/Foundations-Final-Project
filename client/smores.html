S'mores Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 sticks salted butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups cane sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla
  • 2 cups white flour
  • 1 cup crushed graham crackes
  • 1 cup mini semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup dehydrated mini marshmellows
  • 1 cup chopped macadamia nuts
  • 1 package Hershey's mini chocolate bar

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 325*F
  2. Combine eggs, butter, cane sugar and vanilla into mixing bowl.
  3. Mix until creamed together and sugar is no longer granulated.
  4. In a seperate bowl, combine all dry ingredients and mix together.
  5. Slowly add dry ingredients to creamed wet ingredients.
  6. Mix well. Stir in the mini chocolate chips and mini marshmellows.
  7. Use 1 inch cookie scoop and drop onto ungreased pans.
  8. Bake for about 11 minutes or until edges are golden brown.
  9. While still warm, gently press in mini chocolate bar on top of cookie.
  10. Remove from pan and allow to cool on wire baking rack.
