Triple Chocolate Chunk

Ingredients

  • 2 sticks salted butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup cane sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon espresso powder
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla
  • 1 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 cups white flour
  • 1 cup wheat flour
  • 1 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 1 cup milk chocolate chips
  • 1 cup white chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 325*F
  2. Combine eggs, butter, cane sugar, brown sugar and vanilla into mixing bowl.
  3. Mix until creamed together and sugar is no longer granulated.
  4. In a seperate bowl, combine all dry ingredients and mix together.
  5. Slowly add dry ingredients to creamed wet ingredients.
  6. Mix well. Stir in the white, milk and dark chocolate chips.
  7. Use 1 inch cookie scoop and drop onto ungreased pans.
  8. Bake for 11-13 minutes.
  9. Remove from pan and allow to cool on wire baking rack
